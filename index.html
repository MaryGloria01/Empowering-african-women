Our Mission

"Information is akin to power. We're putting it in women's hands."

EAW Founder, DBTWTEi, Nigeria

Many women across Africa have limited access to training, information, and support related to careers. Social and traditional limitations compound this disadvantage from an early age. EAW exists to change that, through free digital education, mentorship, and community.

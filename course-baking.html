Module 5: Healthier Food Options & Baking Tips

Healthier Baking Substitutions:

✅ Use gluten-free flour for sensitive stomachs
✅ Apple sauce or fresh fruits instead of icing for topping
✅ Brown sugar or honey instead of white sugar
✅ Gluten-free baking soda where possible
✅ Olive oil to reduce calorie count

Healthier Nigerian Meal Options:

🌱 Roasted Plantain with Vegetables & Stew, bake plantain 20 mins, prepare vegetable sauce
🥬 Boiled Yam with Vegetables, boil yam with salt, serve with vegetable sauce
🌿 Vegetable Soup with Wheat/Garri, waterleaf, ukazi, stockfish, meat, small palm oil, fry waterleaf separately

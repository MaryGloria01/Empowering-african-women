Oluchi Arinze
Image Consultant & Business Etiquette Coach

Oluchi Arinze is an image consultant and business etiquette coach focused on improving business performance and professional growth. She has a track record of helping professionals improve their social and business skills to build strong personal and corporate reputations. With several years of experience managing top banks' products in Nigeria, she brings real-world expertise to every lesson.

She currently serves as Managing Director of South and Sttes Properties Ltd, Managing Director of South and Sttes Services Ltd, and Director of Kirkwood Properties Ltd.
